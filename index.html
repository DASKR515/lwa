LWA: The Lua Windows Toolkit

Building Lua modules on Windows is broken. We fixed it.

Download Installer (.exe)
90% of LuaRocks issues resolved • MIT Licensed

The Problem

TCC, Clang, and MinGW often fail with complex Lua libraries. I spent months stress-testing alternatives only to find that cl.exe remains the king of stability on Windows.

The Solution

LWA streamlines the Visual Studio toolchain integration, ensuring LuaRocks works out of the box without the usual header/library path headaches.
